Example 6: Uncertainty propagation (track it through conclusions)

Prevent certainty inflation across multi-step reasoning.

Try this prompt

Copy/paste into your LLM. Preserve the labels and constraints.

Context:
A student failed one exam but improved homework scores significantly.

Instruction:
For each conclusion you make:
- State the conclusion
- State the uncertainty level (Low / Medium / High)
- State what additional evidence would reduce uncertainty

Question:
Is the student improving overall?

What this demonstrates

  • Uncertainty tracking across steps (no hidden leaps)
  • Makes assumptions visible and correctable
  • Supports more responsible feedback

Facilitator tip

Introduce a second exam score and see whether uncertainty decreases.
