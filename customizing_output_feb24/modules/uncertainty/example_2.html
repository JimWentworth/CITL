Example 2: Missing-information checklist (gap detection)

Teach the model to identify what it needs before concluding.

Try this prompt

Copy/paste into your LLM. Preserve the labels and constraints.

You are diagnosing why a website is slow.

Known context:
- Page load time is 8 seconds.
- Server CPU usage is normal.

Instruction:
Before answering, list:
1) What is known
2) What is unknown but necessary to determine cause
3) The most likely explanation based on current evidence

Question:
Why is the website slow?

What this demonstrates

  • Gap awareness instead of premature certainty
  • Better next-step questions for troubleshooting
  • Separates diagnosis from data collection

Facilitator tip

Have participants remove one key metric and watch uncertainty increase.
