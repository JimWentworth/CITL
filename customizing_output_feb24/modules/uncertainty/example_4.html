Example 4: Refusal protocol (when not to answer)

Try this prompt

Copy/paste into your LLM. Preserve the labels and constraints.

Reference document:
Water boils at 100°C at sea level.

Instruction:
If the answer is not contained in the reference, respond exactly:
"INSUFFICIENT INFORMATION"

Question:
What is the chemical formula of water?

What this demonstrates

  • Explicit refusal triggered by missing evidence
  • Hard guardrails against hallucination
  • Clean, testable behavior

Facilitator tip

Swap in a question that *is* in the reference to confirm it answers when allowed.
