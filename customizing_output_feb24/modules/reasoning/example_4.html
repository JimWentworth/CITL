Try this prompt

Copy/paste into your LLM. Preserve the step labels and constraints.

You will review a student paragraph.

Focus ONLY on:
- argument structure
- logical coherence
- use of evidence

Ignore entirely:
- grammar
- spelling
- writing style

Output format:
1) One-sentence summary of the argument
2) Two strengths (bullets)
3) Two logical gaps or missing evidence (bullets)
4) One revision suggestion (one sentence)

Student paragraph:
[PASTE TEXT HERE]

What this demonstrates

  • Scope control for evaluation tasks
  • Prevents overreach into irrelevant criteria
  • Improves consistency across graders

Facilitator tip

Add a deliberate grammar error and confirm it doesn’t comment on it.
