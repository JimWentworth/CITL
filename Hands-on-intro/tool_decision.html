Recommended Tool: Microsoft Copilot

Use Copilot when you need fast drafting, rewriting, summarizing, or email support inside the Microsoft ecosystem. It’s ideal for productivity workflows, but not for analyzing long documents end-to-end.

Recommended Tool: Google NotebookLM

Best for long readings, research packets, and student-facing study guides. It uses ONLY the uploaded sources and retrieves relevant chunks, making it the most accurate tool for grounded academic tasks.

Recommended Tool: Google Gemini (Multimodal)

Use Gemini when you need multimodal reasoning — images, charts, diagrams, and accessibility workflows like alt text generation. It “sees” visual input and combines it with text.

Recommended Tool: ChatGPT Projects

Perfect for collaborative curriculum design and iterative drafting. Projects keep your files and instructions active across sessions and support multi-person workflows.
