Best Practices & Tips

Choosing the Right Tool

  • For daily tasks: Microsoft Copilot for integrated productivity
  • For images: Firefly for commercial work, DALL-E 3 (ChatGPT/Copilot) for quick prototypes
  • For research: NotebookLM for synthesis, Gemini for very long documents
  • For audio: ElevenLabs for high-quality voice, ChatGPT voice mode for conversations
  • For video: HeyGen for AI avatars, create assets separately for traditional video editing

Important Considerations

  • Copyright: Check licensing terms, especially for Firefly vs. other image generators
  • Privacy: Don't upload confidential data to public AI tools
  • Verification: Always review AI-generated content for accuracy
  • Attribution: Follow academic integrity policies when using AI tools
  • Voice/Image Rights: Get consent before cloning voices or creating AI videos of real people
