Upskill with Generative AI

Are you interested in learning more about generative AI and how it can support your work? LinkedIn Learning offers a comprehensive Upskilling Framework designed to guide your learning journey, from foundational literacy to specialized technical expertise.

Whether you're looking to understand AI basics, apply AI tools in your daily work, or develop advanced technical skills, there's a pathway for you.

LinkedIn Learning AI Upskilling Framework

Level One Understanding

Featured Skills

AI literacy, generative AI, responsible AI

Who Will Benefit?

For all professionals, including leaders and managers. Includes responsible AI, generative AI fluency, and awareness of AI capabilities and limitations.

Recommended Learning Paths:

  • Career Essentials in Generative AI
  • Building AI Literacy
  • Responsible AI Foundations
Level Two Applying

Featured Skills

Prompt engineering, strategy, copilots and AI pair programming, AI productivity

Who Will Benefit?

For all professionals, including leaders and managers. Includes practical prompt engineering techniques and effective use of AI copilots to enhance productivity.

Recommended Learning Paths:

  • Develop Your Prompt Engineering Skills
  • Microsoft Copilot for Productivity
  • Master GitHub Copilot
  • Build Your Generative AI Productivity Skills
Level Three Building

Featured Skills

No/low-code generative AI, GPTs, APIs, databases

Who Will Benefit?

For power users and developers building AI-powered applications and solutions. Includes low-code methodologies and hands-on practice with APIs.

Recommended Learning Paths:

  • Getting Started with Building Custom GPTs
  • Develop Your Skills with the OpenAI API
  • Hands-On Projects for OpenAI-Powered Apps
  • Building Generative AI Skills for Web Developers
Level Four Training and Maintaining Models

Featured Skills

Building ML models, deep learning, neural networks, NLP, AI tools and libraries

Who Will Benefit?

For engineers implementing AI and machine learning. Includes technical applications of AI and ML models, deep learning architectures, and fine-tuning models.

Recommended Learning Paths:

  • Become an AI Engineer
  • Develop Your Skills for Machine Learning with Python
  • Advance Your Skills in Deep Learning and Neural Networks
  • Advance Your Skills in Natural Language Processing
Level Five Deeply Specializing

Featured Skills

AIOps, MLOps, LLMOps, AI security, AI cloud solutions

Who Will Benefit?

For tech specialists and R&D roles. Includes DevOps engineers, ML researchers, cybersecurity professionals, and data scientists working with advanced AI systems.

Recommended Learning Paths:

  • Manage Your LLMs with LLMOps
  • Develop Your AI Skills as a Cybersecurity Professional
  • Foundational AI Skills for Azure Administration
  • Advance Your Skills in AI and Machine Learning
