The Generative AI Fluency Certificate is a practical, skills-based program designed to equip lifelong learners with the foundational knowledge and strategic capabilities required to responsibly and effectively utilize Generative AI tools in academic, creative, and workplace contexts.

Through a flexible combination of modules and electives, participants explore how AI generates content, practice iterative prompting for idea development, learn to design consistent and ethical outputs, and gain fluency across text, image, and video creation. Hands-on experience with tools like ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Copilot, Firefly, and Notebook LM ensures participants graduate with both confidence and critical awareness in their use of AI.

üìö Core AI Modules

CITL offers a growing collection of AI fluency modules covering essential topics in generative AI. Each module is designed as a self-paced, asynchronous learning experience that combines guided instruction with hands-on practice. Some topics are offered in live online workshops for those who prefer a more structured learning opportunity. Check the CITL Generative AI calendar for upcoming workshops. 

Module topics include:

  • Understanding what generative AI is and what it isn't
  • Collaborative iteration and prompting techniques
  • Designing interactions for consistency
  • Engaging with multimodal AI (text, image, video)
  • AI literacy for students
  • Prompt engineering for teaching and learning
  • Cognitive impacts of generative AI
  • Using and creating custom AI agents

üìù Learning Reflection Project

Throughout the certificate, participants will build a learning journal or portfolio that captures their developing fluency with generative AI. Each module contributes artifacts like prompt examples, AI-assisted drafts, reflections, or multimodal creations that document growth in both skill and awareness.

By the end of the program, the journal serves as both evidence of learning and a personal guide for continued exploration.
