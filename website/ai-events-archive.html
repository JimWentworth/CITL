About Our AI Event Series

The Center for Innovation in Teaching & Learning (CITL) along with campus partners hosted Summer of AI and Fall Into AI at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign. Throughout these series, faculty and staff had the opportunity to participate in hands-on workshops, guest talks, and conversations on how generative artificial intelligence impacts the future of teaching, learning, and beyond.
